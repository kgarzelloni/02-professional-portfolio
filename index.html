About Me

I am forever learning, always on the look out for new skills to develop and expand my personal and professional horizons.
A perfect day for me starts with coffee, time spent hiking in the desert with my family and my dog, followed by some tasty local food and drinks. The way to my heart in through a well paired dinner, wine and dessert combo...and really nice lighting is a plus!
If I'm not spending time with family, I am working on my crafts; coding and fitness. I teach group fitness as a hobby. Give me an hour of your time, and I'll make sure you sweat!
As a gal who hails from the Pacific Northwest, I have found a real fondess for the dry heat of the Sonoran Desert. Tucson has become an easy place for me to call home. Give me allll the stunning sunsets, stately saguaro, and spicy salsa. I'm also a sucker for a good illeratation.
